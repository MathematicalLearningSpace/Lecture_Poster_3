Title: Lemon Pecan Broccoli and Cauliflower Bread 

a & 2 & tbl & Lemon Extract \\
c & 2 & cup of Flour \\
d & 3 & medium & Eggs \\
e & 1.75 & cup & Water \\
f & 3 & tbl &  Honey \\
g & 0.5 & cup & Pecans \\
h & 0.5 & cup & Flax Seeds \\
i & 1 & tbl & Baking Powder \\
j & 5 & packets & Stevia Sweetner \\
k & 1 & cup & Broccoli \\
l & 1 & cup & Cauliflower \\
m & 1 & cup & Zucchini \\
n & 1 & tbl & Basil \\
o & 1 & tbl & Garlic Powder \\
p & 1 & tbl & Onion Powder \\
q & 1 & tbl & Paprika \\

\item Mix A=a,b,c,d,f,h,i,j,k,l
\item Mix e and A with Baker's Ratio for Texture Preference
\item Pour 1/2 of A for  First Layer A in Bread Pan with Coconut Oil Spray
\item Add m to middle of A for Layer B
\item Add n,o,p,q for Layer B
\item Pour 1/2 of A for Layer C
\item Add g,h to Layer C
\item Bake 350 degrees for N minutes Preference 
\item Serve
